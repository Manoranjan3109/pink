About Us

Manoranjan Institute of Technology (MIT) is a premier institution committed to excellence in Engineering, Science, and Technology. With a focus on innovation, research, and practical learning, MIT offers a diverse range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs. Our mission is to develop skilled professionals and industry leaders who make meaningful contributions to society.

At MIT, we provide a dynamic learning environment with cutting-edge facilities, experienced faculty, and strong industry collaborations, ensuring that students gain real-world experience and a competitive edge in their careers.
